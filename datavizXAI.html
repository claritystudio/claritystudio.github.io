Daniele Pretolesi - Short bio

Daniele is an aspiring researcher in the field of Human-Computer Interaction, currently working on his PhD at the Austrian Institute of Technology (AIT).

The main focus of Daniele's research is the role of Extended Realities (XR) in generating user trust in AI applications. Other research areas he is interested in are Human-Robot Interaction and the use of Virtual Reality for the training of law enforcement and medical first responders in complex scenarios.

Before joining AIT, Daniele obtained a Bachelor's degree in Modern Literature at the University of Genoa and a Master's degree in Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence at Tilburg University. During this period, he worked on data-driven digital systems designed to explain and simplify complex textual data, also as part of Clarity Studio.
